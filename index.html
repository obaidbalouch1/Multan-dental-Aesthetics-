Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Does getting veneers hurt?

Most patients feel mild pressure, not pain — the area is numbed, and same-day sensitivity (if any) is usually gone within 24–48 hours.

How many visits does smile design usually take?

A typical veneer case is 2–3 visits: digital planning & preview, preparation, and final fitting. Whitening-only cases can finish in one.

Do you see dental emergencies without an appointment?

We hold same-day emergency slots each day. Call ahead so Dr. Alizay is ready when you arrive.
